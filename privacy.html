Privacy Policy

ScoreArc SAT Coach Internal  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026

ScoreArc SAT Coach Internal ("we", "our", or "the app") is a macOS SAT study app designed to help students focus on the next highest-leverage skill. This policy explains what information the app uses, how it is handled, and your choices.

Information Stored on Your Device

Student progress. The app stores student profiles, question attempts, and coaching history locally on your Mac so it can restore progress between sessions. This information is saved in your device's application support folder and is not uploaded to a developer-operated server by default.

OpenAI API key. If you choose to enable hosted AI coaching, you may supply your own OpenAI API key through an environment variable or a local file on your Mac. That key is read locally by the app and is not provided by us.

Hosted AI Coaching

If you configure the app with your own OpenAI API key, the text you send to the AI coach along with relevant study context such as student name, score goals, recent mistakes, and recommended practice focus may be sent directly from the app to OpenAI to generate a response. If you do not configure an API key, the app uses its built-in local coaching flow instead.

Information We Do Not Collect

ScoreArc SAT Coach Internal does not require account creation. We do not run our own analytics, advertising, or tracking systems for this prototype, and we do not intentionally collect personal information on our own servers through the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. If you choose to use hosted AI coaching, OpenAI processes the prompt content needed to return the coaching response.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because the prototype stores progress locally on your device, you can remove that data by deleting the app's local files or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for students and families preparing for standardized tests. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children on developer-operated servers through this prototype.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
